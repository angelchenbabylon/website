Impressive Insects!

From bees to cockroaches, insects are the most abundant and perhaps the most interesting of all the types of the living organisms on Earth!

There are an estimated 900 000 different species of insects, which is aproximately 80% of all species in the world.
At any time there are an estimated 10 quintillion individual insects.
